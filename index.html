URL parameters

YouTube

append #v=<videoId>&t=<start>;<end> to the current URL and enjoy loop
eg: #v=ZuHZSbPJhaY&=1h1s;1h4s or #v=lWqJTKdznaM&t=58;68&v=ZuHZSbPJhaY&t=1h;1h5s
or even #v=ZNno63ZO2Lw&t=54s;1m20s+1m33s;1m47s+3m30s;3m46s&v=TM1Jy3qPSCQ&t=2s;16s

to import playlist and play nth video: #list=<playlistId>&index=<n>
eg: #list=PLyALKMPGOR5evINIHBRgtioZBuujYFaeS&index=13

Imgur and SoundCloud

use #i=<imgurId>&t=<duration> and #s=<soundCloudPathTo/sound>&t=<start>;<end>
eg: #i=lkD38.gifv&t=5s&s=sacredbones/pharmakon-body-betrays-itself

see more examples at GitHub

keyboard shortcuts

  • spacebar – play/pause player (toggle)
  • k / j – jump to previous/next video (in playlists with multiple videos)
  • h / l – jump to previous/next interval (if a video has more than one)
  • - / + – volume control
  • m – mute video (toggle)
  • x – panic button: mute, pause and hide player (toggle)
  • b – hide player, but keep playing audio in background (toggle)
  • r – jump to random item on playlist
  • e – playlist editor (toggle)
  • s – display short URL for current playlist
  • ? – display help (toggle)
